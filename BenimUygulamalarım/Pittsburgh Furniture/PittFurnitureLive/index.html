Our Goals

To supply, recycle, redistribute, buy, and re-sell furniture of good quality and construct at a reasonable price.

To create a way that is good for self (our store, associates), others (consumers, third parties, etc.), and overall good (via recycling, forest conservation, and all other parties that may be affected).

To work in fairness in a global market for a better future for all those involved and for the environment through an empathetic machinery with a futuristic mindset.

To be always changing for the better with this as our core structure and the want for an overall wellness and enjoyment of all involved.
